Where to stay

There are tons of great accommodations available in NYC, but we are also happy to help you in your search! Consider this a starting list of recommendations.

Our venue is located in Williamsburg (94 Wythe Ave.) near both the L and the G trains, so if maximum ease of transportation to the event is a concern we'd recommend searching for lodging that's within walking distance of either of those train lines.

  • Airbnb - Williamsburg and Greenpoint will probably be the most convenient neighborhoods to search for, but anything in Manhattan south of Central Park or in Brooklyn off of the L or G train lines would be a convenient subway ride away.
  • Wythe Hotel: 80 Wythe Ave. ($$$, but across the street from our venue!)
  • Hotel 17: 225 E. 17th St. ($$)
  • New York Loft Hostel: 249 Varet St. ($)
